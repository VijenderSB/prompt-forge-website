Q: Is LASIK painful?
A: No. Anaesthetic eye drops applied. Zero pain — mild pressure lasting a few seconds.

Q: How long does LASIK last?
A: Permanent corneal reshaping. 95%+ maintain stable vision for decades.

CATEGORY: Eligibility
Q: Am I eligible for LASIK?
A: Adults 18+ with stable prescription (12 months). Myopia -0.5D to -12D, hyperopia +0.5D to +6D, astigmatism up to 6D.
